About UniSelector

UniSelector was founded with a simple mission: to simplify the complex process of university selection. We understand that choosing the right university is one of the most important decisions in a student's life, and our platform is designed to make that process easier, more transparent, and more successful.

Our team of education experts, data scientists, and designers have created a powerful platform that matches students with universities based on their academic profile, preferences, and career aspirations. We've helped thousands of students find their perfect educational path.
